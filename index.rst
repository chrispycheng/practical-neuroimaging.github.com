Practical Neuroimaging Analysis course at UC Berkeley
=====================================================

These are the web pages for the 10-week course on practical neuroimaging
analysis at UC Berkeley `brain imaging center`_ (BIC).

Please join the `mailing list`_

If you need help with installation, or any other course-related thing, we will
be doing office hours at 210 Barker hall from 2pm until 4pm on Tuesday,
Wednesday and Thursday 19, 20, 21st of February.
